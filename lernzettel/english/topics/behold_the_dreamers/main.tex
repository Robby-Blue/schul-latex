\section{Behold The Dreamers} 
\emph{Behold The Dreamers} is a book following the protagonist Jende Jonga and his wife, Neni Jonga, moving from Limbe, Cameroon to NYC. He gets a pretty nice job as a chauffeur for Clark Edwards, working at Lehman Brothers. The book explores the struggles of both the Jonga and the Edwards family.
 
The Jongas struggle with money and the American immigration system, the Edwards struggle with family issues. Both struggle with the collapse of Lehman Brothers. Cindy abuses drugs, Clark starts seeing a prostitute. Clark fires Jende causing Jende to become disillusioned with the American Dream, making him want to return to Limbe. Neni wants to stay. After their relationship continues to worsen, climaxing with him hitting her, they end up moving back.
